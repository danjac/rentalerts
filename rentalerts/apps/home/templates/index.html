Being a foreigner in Helsinki doesn't have to mean being homeless.

Helsinki is one of the most difficult cities in Europe to find affordable housing. Foreigners face special difficulties when moving to Helsinki, especially if they do not have any local connections. Often they end up paying higher rents than Finns.

Helsinki RentAlerts aims to change that by providing a community-driven network to let each other know when properties become available. Whether you need somewhere to live for a few weeks or you are moving here permanently, whether you just need a spare couch or a home for your family, Helsinki RentAlerts is here to help you.
